Services

Since Bill Booth retired from farming in 1980, Booth Family Farms currently leases its’ land to local farmers. Currently, our land is on contract with Lord’s Seed, L.L.C. However, we are still accepting bid offers for land contracts when the current contract is up. We have 220.2 acres of irrigated ground and 46.1 acres farmable dry land for a total of 266.3 farmable acres. Contact us to submit a bid.
